Bio

Aditya is a Sr. Applied Scientist with the AWS Computer Vision team. He completed his PhD in Computer Science studies at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. He was advised by Prof. David Forsyth and Prof. Alexander Schwing. His research areas are Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, GPU Computing.

He TA'ed for CS445 Computational Photography (Fall'17), taught by Prof. Derek Hoiem and CS225 Data Structures (Fall'14) taught by Prof. Cinda Heeren.

Prior to joining University of Illinois, he did his Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science by Research at IIIT Hyderabad, working with Prof. P J Narayanan.

He also manages gpu servers, draws toons, travels, runs, sky-dives, tracks his academic tree in his free time!
